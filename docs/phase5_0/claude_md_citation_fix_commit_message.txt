docs: fix citation — Máša et al. (not Joachimiak et al.) for CSBJ KG-Microbe rule-based paper

The inconsistency was found during manuscript reference verification
while reviewing docs/phase5_0/limitations.md against docs/RELATED_WORK.md.
Both of those correctly cite the CSBJ 2025 KG-Microbe rule-based paper
as "Máša et al. 2025"; docs/CLAUDE.md:287 incorrectly attributed the
same paper to "Joachimiak et al."

The paper is a single three-author paper:
  Petr Máša, Tomáš Kliegr, Marcin P. Joachimiak,
  "Explainable rule-based prediction of cultivation media for microbes,"
  Computational and Structural Biotechnology Journal, Vol 27, 2025,
  pp. 5194-5206. DOI: 10.1016/j.csbj.2025.10.014.
Máša is the first author and Joachimiak the senior/last author; the
standard "et al." form is first-author-based, i.e. "Máša et al." Only
the author attribution on line 287 was changed; the DOI-free
descriptive structure of the line is preserved.

The separate Joachimiak-led citation at docs/CLAUDE.md:281 — the
KG-Microbe knowledge graph preprint (Joachimiak et al. 2025 bioRxiv,
DOI 10.1101/2025.02.24.639989) — is a different paper and is correct;
it was deliberately left unchanged.
