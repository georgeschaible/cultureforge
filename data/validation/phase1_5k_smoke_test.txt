Key outcomes:
1. Nitratidesulfovibrio vulgaris (D. vulgaris): CORRECTLY DETECTED (0.818)
   - Both dsrAB and qmoA present — forward sulfate reducer confirmed

2. Geobacter sulfurreducens: CORRECTLY EXCLUDED (0.400, not detected)
   - Neither dsrAB nor qmoA present — iron reducer, not sulfate reducer
   - No regression from Phase 1.5j

3. Sulfolobus acidocaldarius: CORRECTLY EXCLUDED (0.225, not detected)
   - Has dsrAB but NOT qmoA — dsrAB alone insufficient under AND requirement
   - This is exactly the discrimination Phase 1.5k adds

4. Thermus aquaticus: CORRECTLY EXCLUDED (0.316, not detected)
   - Has dsrAB but NOT qmoA — similar to Sulfolobus case

5. All other organisms: CORRECTLY EXCLUDED
   - No sulfate reduction machinery detected

VERDICT: No regressions. essential_marker_AND working correctly.
D. vulgaris remains the only detected sulfate reducer (correct).
The AND requirement adds qmoA as a discriminator without breaking existing detections.
